Privacy Policy for Next Train Transit

Effective Date: July 31, 2025

Overview

Next Train Transit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Do Not Collect

Next Train Transit is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not store your transit searches or preferences

Transit Information

Next Train Transit displays public transit schedule information from AC Transit. This information is retrieved directly from AC Transit's public API when you use the app. We do not store or cache this transit data on our servers.

No Account Required

Next Train Transit does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.

No Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services in our app.

Data Security

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information to secure. All transit information displayed in the app is publicly available data.

Children's Privacy

Next Train Transit does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13, as we do not collect any personal information at all.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
